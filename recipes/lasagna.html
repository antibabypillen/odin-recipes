Lasagna

Classic homemade lasagna!

Ingredients

  • 1kg ground beef
  • 1 onion and two cloves of garlic
  • one can of crushed tomatoes and two cans of tomato paste
  • two tablespoons of sugar
  • one tablespoon of dried basil
  • fresh parsley
  • salt to taste
  • one tablespoon of italian seasoning
  • one tablespoon of fennel seeds
  • black pepper to taste
  • Store bought lasagna noodles or homemade
  • pick the cheese of your choice
  • one egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. layer the lasagna
  5. cover with foil and bake
  6. let the lasagna rest before serving

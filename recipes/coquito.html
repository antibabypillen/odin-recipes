Coquito

Coquito is a creamy, tropical, Puerto Rican holiday drink!

Ingredients

  • One can of evaporated milk
  • Two beaten egg yolks
  • Canned cream of coconut
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • White rum
  • Water
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cinnamon and cloves
  1. Cook the evaporated milk and egg yolks in a double boiler until thick
  2. Transfer the mixture to a blender and blend with the remaining ingredients
  3. Transfer to glass bottles and refrigerate until cold
